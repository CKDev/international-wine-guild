Level III Certification Options: Guild Wine Master

Basic Certification and Special Emphasis Certification Options

The International Wine Guild, Guild Wine Master (GWM) is an expert level certification. All Guild students who successfully complete the Level II Certification may continue their studies by enrolling in the Level III Certification program - the Guild Wine Master program.

When you enroll in the program you pay for courses as you take them. You may also take individual Level III courses for Continuing Education if you do not desire to earn a Level III Certification. Because each person has a unique education need we have designed the Level III Program to be flexible; you can enroll in the Basic Certification or any one of the different Special Emphasis Certification options.

Each Special Emphasis Certification Option allows you to take courses that better meet your professional or educational needs.

The Guild Wine Master Certification, whether you are completing the Basic Certification or one of the Special Emphasis Certification options, consists of seven (7) courses plus Master Exams for each of the courses you have completed.

Level III Required Core Courses for All Certification Options

  • Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course
  • Advanced Regional France Course
  • Blind Tasting Course
  • All Necessary Master Exams*

* There is a master exam for each Level III course you successfully complete whether you are in the basic or a special emphasis program. Before taking any Master Exams you must first successfully complete the Master Entrance Exam. The Mater Entrance Exam can be taken at any time during your course of study for a Level III Certification.
