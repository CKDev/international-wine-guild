Level 3: Advanced and Master Courses

Level 3 Courses


The following are the educational advanced courses, often called Master courses:

  • Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course.
  • Advanced Regional Course: France
  • Advanced Regional Course: Italy
  • Advanced Regional Course: Europe
  • Advanced Regional Course: New World
  • Advanced Fortified Wines
  • Blind Tasting Course.
  • Wine Business Course
  • International Markets and Management Course
  • History of Wine 1: 8000BC to 1500
  • History of Wine 2: 1500 to present

All of the Level 3 courses are available in either a 10-week format (4 hours, once a week) or a 5-day format (8 hours a day - Monday through Friday).  Both formats teach the same material and you evaluate the same wines.  We offer two formats because half our students are from out-of-state or out-of-country and a multi-week format won't work for them.

Contact the Guild for details.
