An in-depth knowledge of Italian wine is critical to the serious wine student. Italy is the largest wine producing country in the world (just beating out France) and a key wine exporter to the United States. 

Taste 40+ Italian wines while taking a deep dive into Italian geography and terrior, wine production techniques by style and region and much more.

 

 

This course covers:

  • Italy geography and terroir
  • Italian wine production techniques by style and region
  • Italian wine growing and obligation requirements for each Appellation in the regions studied
  • Italian wine character data, by appellation
  • Tastings of 50+ Italian wines
  • 850+ pages of educational material, designed specifically for this course

This course covers the following areas in Italy:

  • NE Italy (3 regions)
  • NW Italy (4 regions)
  • Central Italy (7 regions)
  • Southern Italy & Islands (6 regions)

This course is available in two formats, with no difference in course content or instruction:

  • 10-Week Course, meeting once a week: Class meets from 6 PM - 10 PM once a week for 10 weeks and a total of 40 contact hours; Course fee: $1,900 (includes registration fees, handouts, 50+ Italian wine tastings, and instruction)
  • 5-Day Intensive Study Course, meeting daily: Class meets from 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM daily, Monday through Friday, for a total of 40 contact hours; Course fee: $1,975 (includes registration fees, handouts, 65+ older vintage Italian wine tastings, lunch each day, and instruction)
