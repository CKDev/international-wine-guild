Level 1 Seminars: Wine and Fine Spirits

The International Wine and Spirits Guild offers two basic seminars, which individually act as introductory courses of study designed for individuals who desire to learn about wine and spirits.

Please select a Level I seminar below to learn more about what each class entails:

  • Wine Seminar: The basic Guild Wine Seminar is an introductory  course that introduces France, Italy and the United States as well as technical descriptive evaluation and comparative evaluation.   

  • Fine Spirits Seminar: The Guild Fine Spirits Seminar is designed to introduce enthusiasts to fine spirits. That is, spirits usually consumed neat or straight. Students learn about fine spirits production, evaluation, and spirit and food pairing.
