Level III: International Wine Markets and Management Course

View Dates, Locations and Register for This Wine Class

This wine business course focuses on timely topics in the international wine industry, including wine economics and the current state of wine markets around the world. 

Topics Covered:

  • Wine Economics
  • The World Wine Economy
  • Financial Planning & Analysis for the Wine Industry
  • International Wine Markets & Analyzing Wine Markets at Different Levels
  • Wine Marketing
  • Wine Business Marketing Plan & Advertising Program

You will taste 20 wines and receive 400+ pages of material, specifically designed for this course.

Prerequisites: 

This class is open to all, but we strongly recommend that you first take the Guild Certification Seminar before attempting any Level III course.

This is a required course for the Level III Certification. You may be enrolled in our Level II Certification and concurrently take this course. 

This course is the second of our wine business courses, and is a more detailed and technical course than our Wine Business course.
