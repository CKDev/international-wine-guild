IWG Wine School Texas

The International Wine Guild is excited to provide our professional and continuing education wine seminars and courses in Austin, Dallas, Johnson City and Houston through our marketing partners.

You don't have to be a professional to attend our seminars and courses.  Half our students are typically enthusiasts.

Texas Wine Classes: Austin • Dallas • Johnson City • Houston

Level 1 Seminar Award Exam

For any of our Level 1 Wine Seminars you can sign-up for the award exam either before or after you attend the seminar. If you sign-up for the exam and take it within two weeks of taking the seminar use the following registration:

Register: Level I Wine or Spirits Seminar On-Line Exam ($50) - TX  
Take the exam at your convenience.  When you sign up we will send you the information you will need to take the exam. 

If you are taking the exam more than 2 weeks after you attended the seminar, use this registration:

Register: Level I Wine or Spirits Seminar On-Line Exam ($100) - After 2 weeks - TX  
Take the exam at your convenience.  When you sign up we will send you the information you will need to take the exam. 

When you sign up for the on-line exam the Guild will send you an email with instructions on how to take the exam and a second email, from the company we use to administer the exam, inviting you to their website to the take the exam.

PLEASE NOTE: You must register for courses through the International Wine and Spirits Guild. Online registration closes 1 day prior to the seminar or course start date. If you need to register after the close date, please contact the International Wine & Spirits Guild at 303-296-3966. If you have any questions please call the same number.

Because our seminars and courses are extremely expensive to produce, a cancellation by a student within ten (10) days of the start of the Seminar would result in a non-refundable fee of $350.00.  A student cancelling three (3) days prior to the start of a seminar or short-course will mean a forfeiture of the entire registration fees. There is a $150 fee for transferring from one seminar to another. If you transfer from one seminar to another you will still owe $495 for the seminar to which you are transferring.

 

This also applies to our Level 2 and Level 3 courses, a cancellation by a student within ten (10) days of the start of the Course would result in a non-refundable fee of $1200.00.  A student cancelling three (3) days prior to the start of a seminar or short-course will mean a forfeiture of the entire registration fees. There is a $250 fee for transferring from one seminar to another. If you transfer from one seminar to another you will still owe $1900 (or $1995) for the course to which you are transferring.

 

If for any reason a class is canceled we will phone or e-mail you 48 hours prior to the start of the class. If a class is canceled at the last minute due to bad weather (this is very rare) we will reschedule the class. However, if a class is held and you decide not to attend because you believe the weather is bad then it will be treated as a no-show.

It is highly recommended that you register early, your credit card will not be charged until ten days in advance of the seminar or course. We must have a minimum students registered in order to form a course. If you are traveling from out of town to attend, please do not make travel arrangements until two weeks in advance of the course.
