Intensive Study Advanced One Week Courses

For those who are looking for a shorter version of our 10-week advanced courses we offer the Level II Certification course and all of the Level III Certification courses as one-week intensive study programs. All of these courses are ideal for anyone who would like an advanced certification but does not live in the Denver area.

You do not need to be in the trade to take any of our professional courses. If you are a serious enthusiast then you can participate in any of our professional courses. Or, if you are considering a career change, then our professional courses will provide you with the education to make the jump to the wine industry.

Professional programs of the International Wine & Spirits Guild wine school are approved and regulated by the Colorado Department of Higher Education, Private Occupational School Board.

Intensive Study Advanced Wine Course (Level II Certification)

This is a seven day (60 contact hour) course. Those who successfully complete the written and practical exams for this course will receive a Level II Certification diploma.

The cost for registration, course materials, executive certification and wine is $1,975. This advanced course is offered each quarter as either a 10-week or 1-week intensive study course.

Click this link if you would like more information on the Intensive Study Advanced Wine Course.

Intensive Study 1-Week Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course (A Level III Course)

This is a five day (44 contact hour) course that covers all of the topics as the 10-Week Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course.

You do not have to be in the trade to take any Level III professional courses. However, we strongly recommend you take the Guild Certification Seminar prior to any Level III Course.

The cost for registration, course materials, executive/senior certification, food (a lunch and early dinner each day) and wine is $2,000. This advanced course is offered each quarter as an 10-week course and twice a year as a 1-week intensive study course.

Click this link if you would like more information on the Intensive Study Advanced Wine and Food Pairing Course.

Intensive Study Advanced Regional Courses (Level III Courses)

Each of these are five day courses that covers all of the same material as our 10-week college level Advanced Regional Courses. There are three advanced regional courses: France, Italy and the New World.

You do not have to be in the trade to take any Level III professional courses. However, we strongly recommend you take the Guild Certification Seminar prior to any Level III Course.

The cost for registration, course materials, food (lunch each day) and wine is $1,975. Each of these three advanced regional courses is offered twice a year as an 10-week course and twice a year as a 1-week intensive study course.

Click this link if you would like more information on the Intensive Study Advanced Regional Wine Courses.

Intensive Study Blind Tasting Course (a Level III Course)

This is a five day (44 contact hour) course that covers all of the topics as the 11-Week Blind Tasting Course.

We require that you have taken the Level II Advanced Wine Course prior to attempting this course.

The cost for registration, course materials, food (a lunch each day) and wine is $2,075. This also includes taking the Level III entrance exam. This advanced course is offered twice a year as an 10-week course and once a year as a 1-week intensive study course.

The cost for registration, course materials,food (a lunch each day) and wine is $1,975. This advanced course is offered twice a year as an 10-week course and once a year as a 1-week intensive study course.
