How to Launch Your Wine Career by Liz Thach, Ph.D. and Brian D’Emillio
ISBN: 978-934259-06-1. 2011

Hating life in a cubicle where the only greenery is that of the mold growing on the rungs of the corporate ladder? Have a dream of being in the wine industry?

The US is the largest wine-consuming market in the world. Get your foot in the door now and watch your career grow with the help of these two industry icons!

How to Launch Your Wine Career is the first book of its kind. This book provides practical, real-world advice on how to land, develop and succeed in the business of wine from wine making and production to vineyard management and sales; wine education and teaching to importing and distributing; to wine writing and consulting.

The appendix provides a detailed “Action Plan Worksheet” to help the prospective applicant plan, plot progress and nail that golden wine industry job.
