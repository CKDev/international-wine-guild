Class Schedule

The International Wine Guild calendar of upcoming seminars and courses is below. This is a list of all of our seminars and courses and connects you to web pages for each of them

Level 1 Wine Seminar or Level 1 Spirits Seminar.

The Level 1 seminars are fundamental, beginning education. All of our courses begin with the Guild Level 1 Wine Seminar or the Guild Level 1 Spirits Seminar. Each of our Level 1 Seminars is a 12-hour, day and a half (Saturday-Sunday) seminar. The wine seminar is offered the first weekend of every month in Denver as well as other locations. Check the schedule for dates and locations.

The Level 1 Spirits Seminar is offered 4 times a year. Check the schedule for dates and locations.

Level 2 Advanced Wine Course or Level 2 Advanced Fine Spirits Course

The Level 2 courses build upon the foundation established in the Level 1 Wine or Spirits seminar. These are intermediate level courses. 

There are two ways to take the Level 2 Wine Course:

  • Advanced Wine Course (11-week).
  • Intensive Study Advanced Wine Course (6-Day).

For those that don't live in Colorado we recommend the Intensive Study Advanced Wine Course (6-Day). The course content, wine tasting and instruction are identical in the 11-Week and 6-Day courses.

The Level 2 Advanced Fine Spirits Course is also an intermediate level course.  It is available in two formats: (a0 10-Week and (b) 5-Day.

To learn more about either Level 2 course go to the following hyperlinks:

  • Advanced Wine Course
  • Advanced Fine Spirits Course

Master level courses are advanced courses that are a step above the Level 2 intermediate courses. 

You can register for any of the courses, however, we recommend, but don't require, that you go through the Level 1 Wine Seminar first. Go to the following hyperlinks to learn more about our Level 3 courses.
