International Wine & Spirits Guild Gift Certificates

Our gift certificates make the perfect holiday gift for wine enthusiasts and professionals!

Gift certificates can be used to:

  • Purchase wine maps, books, and sommelier tools in the Guild Marketplace
  • Savor some of the world's most famous wine and food pairings at our Guild Dinners
  • Attend wine and fine spirits course (includes wine/spirits tastings, educational material, and a gourmet lunch) at any of our 9 locations across the U.S.

Contact the Guild directly at 303.296.3966 or info@internationalwineguild.com for a gift certificate denomination not listed below.

You will be able to print a receipt at the time of purchase.

Please allow 24-48 hours for your gift certificate to arrive via email. Gift certificates are valid for one full year from the date of purchase.
