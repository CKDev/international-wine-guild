Level 3 Advanced Regional Europe Course

Germany, Austria, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Hungary and more


Advanced Regional Europe

This course is a complement to our other Advanced Regional courses: France, Italy and New World.

You will learn:

  • Geography and terroir
  • Wine production techniques by style and region
  • How to use vintage data, by region
  • Wine growing and obligation requirements for each Appellation in the regions studied

This course covers the following countries:

  • Switzerland
  • Austria
  • Germany
  • Hungary
  • Bulgaria and Eastern Europe
  • Greece
  • Portugal
  • Spain

You will receive more than 500 pages of material, specifically designed for this course and taste 50+ wines, including a few older vintage wines.
