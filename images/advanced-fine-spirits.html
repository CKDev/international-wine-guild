Level 2 Advanced Fine Spirits

A Level 2 Course


Fine spirits have always been associated with those who enjoy fine wines. This advanced course will present information on the history, styles, production, and tasting etiquette of some of the worlds finest spirits.

You will network with other fine spirits enthusiasts,  taste 25+ different spirits, and receive 400+ pages of educational material specifically designed for this course.

  • Gin
  • Absinthe
  • Scotch Whisky and Irish Whiskey; Japanese Whisky
  • Bourbon and other American Whiskeys
  • Rum and Cane Spirits
  • Sipping Tequila, Mescal and Sokol
  • Vodka
  • Key Liqueurs

You will also learn about:

  • Sommelier service for spirits and liqueurs
  • Tasting etiquette
  • Technical tasting techniques for different spirits
  • The history and development of spirits
  • Spirit fermentation, distillation, and production techniques
  • Tasting neat and straight
